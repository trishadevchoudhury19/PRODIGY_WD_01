Our Gym Facilities

Cardio Area

Our cardio area is equipped with top-of-the-line treadmills, stationary bikes, and elliptical trainers to help you improve cardiovascular health and burn calories.

Weightlifting Section

In our weightlifting section, you'll find a variety of free weights and weight machines suitable for all fitness levels. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced lifter, we have the equipment you need to build strength and muscle.

Group Exercise Classes

We offer a range of group exercise classes led by certified instructors. From yoga and pilates to high-intensity interval training (HIIT) and Zumba, there's something for everyone.

Swimming Pool

Cool off and enjoy a low-impact workout in our indoor swimming pool. We offer lap swimming, water aerobics classes, and swimming lessons for all ages and skill levels.

Sauna and Steam Room

Relax and unwind after your workout in our sauna and steam room. These amenities are perfect for soothing sore muscles and promoting relaxation.
